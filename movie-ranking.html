The Best Movies According to Ankitha

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Darling

It's a beautiful love story. Songs and Background music made me cherish this movie forever. Prabhas and Kajal nailed it. Whenever I watch this film, it always gives me a fresh feel.

Arundhati

It's a female-oriented film. It's an excellent and scary movie. The Concept is entirely different. The climax was superb. I feel nostalgic whenever I watch this movie. Anushka and Sonu Sood did justice for their roles. They nailed it.

Godavari

It's a feel good movie. Sita and Ram characters are lovable with natural expressions. I enjoyed the scenic beauties of River Godavari and surrounding places.
